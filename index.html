MY CORE VALUES

Loyalty

Loyalty as a personal value means being committed and dependable in your relationships, beliefs, and responsibilities, even when it’s difficult. It involves standing by the people you care about, keeping your promises, and staying true to your principles without betraying trust. At the same time, healthy loyalty isn’t blind—it includes honesty and knowing your limits, so you can support others while still respecting your own values and well-being.

Where Does Loyalty Derive From?

Loyalty comes for wanting personal experiences, and values that we learn overtime. Becuase humans are social beings, people naturaly form bonds and depend on one another, which creates a basic drive to stay connected and commited. It's also shaped by upbringing--family, culture, and life experiences teach us the importance of trust and reliability.
